In a Decade

In Ten Years, many coastal cities around the world
will start being submerged under water
because of Global Warming. Povery will increase,
there will be increase in population,
decrease in space around the globe and scarcity of resources.
Inflation will be at all-time high.

In a Quadranscentennial

In Twenty Five years, all the major governments will collapse and there will be widespread protests and hunger strikes accross the world. Then, a single group of individuals will come together to work together, to bring everyone to equality.

In a Semicentennial

In Fifty Years, there won't be any borders around the globe, a group of individuals will take over the United Nations. Immediate issues like Global Warming and Poverty will be over.

In a Centennial

In Hundred Years, all the main problems of the world such as global warming, terrorism, racism will cease to exist. The technology has developed a lot, and the greenery has returned. The waters are cleaner, the air is cleaner.
